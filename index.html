Since November 2022, I am a Ph.D. student in Information Technology at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering of Politecnico di Milano. I received my Master's degree in Mathematical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano in 2021. After a period as a research fellow in the AIRLab research team, I started my Ph.D. supervised by Prof. Marcello Restelli. My main research topics revolve around Reinforcement Learning, with a particular focus on the world of Representation Learning for RL. My research activities are related to both fundamental and industrial research.
